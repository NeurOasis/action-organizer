🧠 Key Considerations for Zach

  • Check for events: Call ahead or check websites to avoid unexpected crowds or noise (like that concert at the trailhead)
  • Plan for screen breaks: iPad breaks are not just allowed but encouraged as regulation tools
  • Keep activities short: 1-2 hours max, with flexibility to leave early if needed
  • Have backup plans: Indoor alternatives for every outdoor activity
  • Morning advantage: Start outdoor activities early before crowds arrive
  • Emphasize choice: Let Zach help choose between 2-3 pre-planned options
  • Nintendo connections: Frame activities in terms he loves (Mario Kart timing on scooter, collecting things like power-ups)
